Welcome!

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Economics at the University of Rochester.
I will be on the job market in 2024-2025.

My research primarily focuses on the determinants of earnings dynamics. In my job market paper, I study the role of risk aversion heterogeneity in occupation choices and resulting earnings dynamics using a structural model of dynamic occupation choice as well as regression analysis.

In other words, I examines the causal effect of labor input regulation on technology adoption in the context of the healthcare sector.
